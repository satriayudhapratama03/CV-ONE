FAQS

DOES CV ONE ACCEPT ORDERS FROM INDIVIDUALS OR PERSONAL?

No, CV ONE only serves Business to Business (B2B) transactions. Orders must be made through a legally registered company with valid business documentation.

WHAT ARE THE REQUIREMENTS TO PLACE ORDER WITH CV ONE?

Your company must provide legal business documents for verification. Once approved, orders can be placed with a minimum quantity of 5 tons per order.

CAN I PURCHASE A SMALL QUANTITY OF TEA FOR PERSONAL USE OR SAMPLING?

Unfortunately, no. CV ONE does not provide retail or personal-use quantities. All orders must be meet the minimum 5-ton requirement and be for business purpose.

I’M AN INDIVIDUAL PLANNING TO START A TEA BUSINESS, CAN I COLLABORATE WITH CV ONE?

Yes, but you must first establish a legally registered company. CV ONE only collaborates with verified businesses to ensure proper distribution to ensure proper ditribution and partnership.
